# Canonical Map  
With a [Group](./Group.md) $G$ and a [Factor%20Group%20(Modulo)](./Factor%2520Group%2520(Modulo).md) $G/H$,  
The [Map](../Mapping/Map.md) of $G\rightarrow G/H$ is called the Canonical Map.
