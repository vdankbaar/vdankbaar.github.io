# Canonical Map  
With a [Group](./Group.md) $G$ and a [Factor Group (Modulo)](./Factor20Group2028Modulo29.md) $G/H$,  
The [Map](../Mapping/Map.md) of $G\rightarrow G/H$ is called the Canonical Map.
