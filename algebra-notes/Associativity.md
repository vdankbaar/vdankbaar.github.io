# Associativity  
Refers to the sequence in which the operators are applied.  
A [Law of composition](./Law-of-composition.md) is associative if for $x$, $y$ and $z$ in [Set](./Sets/Set.md) $S$:  
$$x(yz)=(xy)z$$
