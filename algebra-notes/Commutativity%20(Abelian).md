# Commutativity (Abelian)  
Refers to the sequence of the inputs.  
A [Law%20of%20composition](./Law%2520of%2520composition.md) is commutative if for $x$ and $y$ in [Set](./Sets/Set.md) $S$:  
$$xy=yx$$
